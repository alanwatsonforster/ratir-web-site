RATIR Closed for the Winter Break

14 December 2018

RATIR closed today for the OAN winter break and will reopen in the week of 7 January 2019.

RATIR Open Again

11 January 2018

The RATIR cryostat has been pumped down and cooled. RATIR is now observing again with C0, C1, and C2. As usual, C3 is taking some time to settle after cooling.
