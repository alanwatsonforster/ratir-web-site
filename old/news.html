RATIR Closed Due To COVID-19

21 March 2020

The Observatorio Astronómico Nacional is being evacuated due to the risk of COVID-19. RATIR will be closed until further notice.

C2 and C3 Operational Again

12 March 2020

From 5 to 11 March, Wilson Lara (GSFC), Alan Watson (UNAM), and Javier Hernández Landa (UNAM) serviced the cryostat cold head. It appears to be working correctly again, and we hope the cryostat will now give its usual long hold times.

The C2 and C3 detectors are operational again.

RATIR Open after the Winter Break

6 January 2020

The Observatorio Astronómico Nacional has resumed operations after the winter break. RATIR has opened again.

The C2 and C3 detectors continue to be not operational.
