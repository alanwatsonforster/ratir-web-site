RATIR: News for 2019

  • RATIR Closed for the Winter Break
  • C2 and C3 Not Operational
  • RATIR Open after the Winter Break

RATIR Closed for the Winter Break

14 December 2019

RATIR closed today for the OAN winter break and will reopen in the week of 6 January 2020.

C2 and C3 Not Operational

01 November 2019

The cryostat has shown increasingly show hold times since summer of 2019, and now only holds for a few days. Pumping and cooling the cryostat after each warm up takes several days. We have therefore taken the decision to no longer attempt to cool the cryostat until we can service the cold head.

As a result, the C2 and C3 detectors will not be operational until further notice.

RATIR Open after the Winter Break

12 January 2019

The OAN has resumed operations after the winter break. The RATIR cryostat has been pumped down and cooled. RATIR is now open again and observing with C0, C1, and C2.
